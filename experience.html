My first 48hr long Hackathon
May 4, 2026

Codefest

Sungava Collage,Parsa,Chiwtan,NEPAL

It was my 2nd hackathhon of my life. It ws a 48hr non stop hackathon organized by Codefest Nepla. Here me and my team we went on the morning of the hackathon and we did registrationa nd went insid ethe veneu and met with the peoples and other we all went tohave breakfast and join others.

The hackathon started at 12:00 PM, and since it was open-themed, we decided to build a web app aimed at helping farmers. Our main idea was to make something practical and impactful. One of our most exciting features was an AI system that could analyze crops and determine whether they were healthy or affected by any disease. We trained our model using thousands of images and managed to achieve around 90% accuracy, which we were really proud of. Alongside that, we also built a feature that helps users find nearby fertilizer shops, complete with detailed pricing and map locations. The goal was simple — make farmers’ lives easier and help them get better results from their crops. After a long day of building, we went out for dinner and enjoyed some great food. Some of us went to sleep early, while others stayed up all night working and chatting. It was a fun and memorable experience. The next morning, we had breakfast and headed to the presentation area. We presented our project to the judges and other participants. We received valuable feedback and even made some great connections with others at the event. Overall, it was an amazing experience. We learned a lot, built something meaningful, and had a great time. We’re definitely looking forward to participating in more hackathons in the future.
